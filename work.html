An innovative product that leaves users wanting more

There are a lot of options for getting around San Francisco. Each day thousdands of people use their bikes, public transportation, taxi services, or their own vehicle to get around. Biking is a great thing and I admire people who treck up and down the San Francisco hills in the name of exercise and the environment, all of you are great people. As noble as biking can be, it’s not always the best mode of transportation. Showing up to a shower-less office isn’t always the most enjoyable experience and let’s not forget to daily ritual of patting down the lower back and armpits with copious amounts of paper towels.
